Oner Express Ug - Site Plan

Site Name

Oner Express Ug — A website designed to provide information about online markets, affiliate products, and consumer deals in Uganda. I chose this name because it clearly represents the focus on Oner Express Ug and makes the site recognizable and easy to brand.

Optional domain availability: OnerExpressUg.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as a hub for Ugandan consumers and entrepreneurs by providing:

  • Guides on finding the best deals online.
  • Affiliate product recommendations and reviews.
  • Educational content on e-commerce and digital business growth.

Scenarios

Examples of questions users might ask:

  • “Where can I find trusted affiliate product recommendations in Uganda?”
  • “How can I start selling my products online?”

Color Schema

The site will use the following color palette:

  • Primary Color (Dodger Blue): Used for headings, navigation links, and call-to-action buttons.
  • Secondary Color (Gold): Used for highlights, accents, and important notices.
  • Background: Light gray/off-white for readability.
  • Text: Dark gray for body content.

Typography

  • Headings: Merriweather, serif (for elegance and readability).
  • Body Text: Roboto, sans-serif (for clean, modern look).
  • Accents/Navigation: Roboto Bold.
