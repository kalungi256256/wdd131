Methodology Notes

Carbon Footprint Calculations: All emission factors used in the calculator are based on widely accepted scientific studies and government sources. Regional variations may apply, and the calculator provides estimates for educational and awareness purposes.

Eco Tips: Sustainability recommendations are compiled from peer-reviewed research and established environmental organizations. Impact estimates are based on average household data and may vary based on individual circumstances.

Data Sources: No personal data is collected or stored on external servers. All user data remains on the local device using browser localStorage functionality.
