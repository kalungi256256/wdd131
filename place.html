Uganda

The Pearl of Africa

About

Uganda, known as the "Pearl of Africa," is celebrated for its lush landscapes, abundant wildlife, and welcoming people. It is home to half of the world's mountain gorillas, found in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. The country is also the source of the Nile River and features diverse ecosystems, from the snow-capped Rwenzori Mountains to the expansive Lake Victoria.
